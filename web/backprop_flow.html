The three identities

Forward: Z = XW + b, A = σ(Z). Backward, given G = ∂L/∂A:

∂L/∂Z = G ⊙ σ′(Z) — saturation kills gradient
∂L/∂X = (∂L/∂Z) Wᵀ — (n×k)(k×d) → (n×d)
∂L/∂W = Xᵀ (∂L/∂Z) — a sum of rank-1 votes, one per example
