The ε–δ dial

Width fights the size of collisions (each row's expected excess ≤ εt/e); depth fights the luck of collisions (min over d independent rows; all bad at once with probability ≤ e−d ≤ δ). Estimates never come in under the truth — the lie is one-sided, just like the Bloom filter's.
