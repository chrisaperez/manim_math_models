Why exactly k layers

After k rounds, hv is a function of v's k-hop neighborhood — nothing else. These two graphs agree on every tree of depth ≤ 3 around red; the cycle-closing edge vs. the dangling leaves live 3–4 hops out, so no 3-layer GNN can ever separate them, and a 4-layer one must.

Hovering a node shades everything within k hops (its current receptive field). Rewiring an edge re-runs the trace — try deleting the cycle edge and watch the divergence depth move.
