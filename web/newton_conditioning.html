The cost ledger

GD: O(d) per step × O(κ·log 1/ε) steps.
Newton: O(d³) per step (solve H d = −∇J) × O(1) steps near the optimum, affine-invariant — rotating or stretching the problem changes nothing.
BFGS: O(d²) per step, curvature learned from gradient differences via the secant condition — the practical middle (HW8.4).
