What the meter is telling you

With no dead ends, every column of M sums to 1, so one step r′ = Mr just rearranges the mass (his HW3 1(a) proof: swap the order of summation). A dead end is a zero column — its rank pours out of the system entirely. A spider trap conserves mass but never gives it back. Teleportation redistributes (1−β) of everything uniformly each step: the meter pins at 1 and no structure can capture the surfer.

Convergence is geometric with rate ≈ β: lower β converges faster but trusts the link structure less.
